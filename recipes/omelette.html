Description

French omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheese (any kind)
  • 3 to 4 cherry tomatoes [1], cut in half and sprinkled lightly with salt
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh herbs (optional)

Steps:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add the butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted [1], add the whisked eggs and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the grated cheese and tomatoes, and cook for another minute.
  5. Flip the omelette and cook until golden brown.
  6. Sprinkle with salt and herbs (optional), and serve.
