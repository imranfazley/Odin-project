Microwave Baked Potato

Ingredients

  • 1 large (10 to 12-ounce) russet potato
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil, optional
  • Pinch of kosher salt, optional

Steps:

  1. Prepare to potato
  2. Microwave the potato
  3. Crisp the skin
  4. Serve the potato
